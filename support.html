House of Cards Support

House of Cards helps families scan, organize, and value trading cards across Pokemon and football collections.

Use the support tracker below to report bugs, ask questions, or share scanning issues.

Need help?

Open a ticket on the House of Cards support tracker and include the device you are using, the card or screen involved, and what happened.

Suggested troubleshooting details

  • Whether the issue happened during camera capture, chosen-photo import, or saved-photo reprocess
  • Whether cloud save was enabled
  • The card name, set, or sport you were working with
  • A screenshot if the issue is visual
