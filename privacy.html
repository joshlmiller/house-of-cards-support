Card Den

Privacy Policy

This policy explains what Card Den stores on your device, what can sync through cloud save, and how family account data is handled.

Questions about this policy can be submitted through the public support tracker linked below.

What the app stores

Card Den stores card details, notes, condition, pricing history, and scanned photos on the device first so the app can keep working even if cloud save is unavailable.

What syncs when cloud save is enabled

If you turn on cloud save, the app can sync card details, scanned photos, family member collections, storage locations, smart collections, and approval records through the app's Supabase backend so the same household can use more than one device.

Camera and photo access

The app requests camera access to scan trading cards. If you choose a saved photo instead, the app only uses the image you select for recognition and storage.

Family accounts

Family accounts can separate collections by member and can protect family management actions with a parent PIN. Parent authorization is verified against the backend when required.

Third-party services

Card Den uses third-party card data and valuation providers to identify Pokemon cards and fetch market pricing. The app does not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

Delete your data

You can remove local data by deleting the app. If cloud save is enabled, the app's account management flow includes a delete-account action that removes the synced account, family member collections, and cloud photos.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, use the Card Den support tracker.
